# Business Requirements

## BR1: Web-based Administrative Management System
The client requires a scalable and reliable platform that enhances live sports engagement while providing administrators with professional tools to manage predictions, advertising, and game content in one place. This requirement reflects the business need to move beyond mobile-only solutions and establish a structured system that improves user interaction, creates revenue opportunities through integrated advertising, and streamlines operational workflows. By offering a centralized and future-ready platform, the project supports both long-term growth and sustainability for the client’s business model.

## BR2: Live operations reliability
Live sports products fail in the market when operators cannot keep pace with the broadcast. The admin experience must support fast, repeatable workflows (templates, hotkeys, clear session state) so a small operations team can run a high-quality show under time pressure.

## BR3: Trust, safety, and brand risk management
As audience chat and messaging features expand, the business needs practical moderation and visibility tools so harmful content can be addressed quickly without derailing the core game experience.

## BR4: Accountability and post-incident review
The business needs traceability for operational changes (who changed what, and when), especially as more admins collaborate across sessions and templates.

## BR5: Analytics-ready exports
Partners and internal teams need to analyze gameplay and operational outcomes outside the live UI. Exports (CSV/JSON) support reporting, tuning, and partner deliverables without requiring bespoke engineering for every question.

## BR6: Faster onboarding for new operators
The platform should reduce training time through clearer workflows, previews of player-facing UI, and optional in-product guidance (including optional AI assistance when configured).

## BR7: Sustainable engineering velocity
The architecture should keep domains separated (games, predictions, ads, messaging/moderation, exports, audit) so new features can ship without destabilizing unrelated areas of the product.
